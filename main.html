About us

The Davidbek Games is an annual competition and festival organized by the public organization "Davidbek Games", which unites military-patriotic organizations, educational institutions and youth teams of Armenia. The event is notable for the importance of military-patriotic education, encouragement of physical fitness and appreciation of cultural values.

Our goals:

  • To foster the patriotic spirit of society
  • Promote physical fitness and tactical skills of young people
  • Strengthen respect for cultural, spiritual and historical values
  • To create a platform for cooperation between military-patriotic organizations, educational institutions and sports teams
  • Encourage interest in the historical and cultural heritage of Syunik

Games 2022-2025

2022: Our first games were held at the Baghaberd camp in Kapan, involving 8 teams.
2023: The second games were held at the Vanek camp in Kapan (henceforth the permanent camp site of the Davitbek Games) with the participation of 10 teams.
2024: The event expanded to host 12 teams.
